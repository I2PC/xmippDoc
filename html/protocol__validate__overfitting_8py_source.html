44 class XmippProtValidateOverfitting(ProtReconstruct3D):
45  """
46  Check how the resolution changes with the number of projections used for
47  3D reconstruction.
48 
49  NOTE:
50  Using the output plot, with the reconstruction of aligned gaussian noise,
51  you can assess the validity of the reconstruction from your micrograph
52  images. Practically, if the resolution of reconstruction based on your
53  images is not considerably different from aligned gaussian noise one
54  (for less number of particles),your images may not produce a valid
55  reconstruction.
56 
57  This method has been proposed by:
58  B. Heymann "Validation of 3D EM Reconstructions", 2015.
59  (see References)
60  """
