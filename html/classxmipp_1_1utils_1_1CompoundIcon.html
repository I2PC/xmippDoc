Detailed Description

The CompoundIcon will paint two, or more, Icons as a single Icon.

The Icons are painted in the order in which they are added.

The Icons are laid out on the specified axis:

  • X-Axis (horizontally)
  • Y-Axis (vertically)
  • Z-Axis (stacked)
