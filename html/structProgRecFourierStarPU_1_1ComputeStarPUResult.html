◆ volumeData

std::complex<float>* ProgRecFourierStarPU::ComputeStarPUResult::volumeData = nullptr

3D volume holding the cropped (without high frequencies) Fourier space. Lowest frequencies are in the center, i.e. Fourier space creates a sphere within a cube.

On GPU reinterpreted as array of float2, or float with each two successive values represent one complex number.
