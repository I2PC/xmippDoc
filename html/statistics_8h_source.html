599 /*************************************************************************
600 Pearson's correlation coefficient significance test
601 
602 This test checks hypotheses about whether X and Y are samples of two
603 continuous distributions having zero correlation or whether their
604 correlation is non-zero.
605 
606 The following tests are performed:
607  * two-tailed test (null hypothesis - X and Y have zero correlation)
608  * left-tailed test (null hypothesis - the correlation coefficient is
609  greater than or equal to 0)
610  * right-tailed test (null hypothesis - the correlation coefficient is
611  less than or equal to 0).
